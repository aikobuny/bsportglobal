About Us

BSPORT GLOBAL, incorporated on November 8, 2023, and also known as BSPORT GOLF, is a newly established company specializing in corporate events, golf tournaments, and golf development. Although a recent entrant, BSPORT is focused on becoming the leading organizer of corporate golf events, followed by junior and amateur golf tournaments, in Malaysia. Through its Junior Golf Academy, BSPORT is dedicated to nurturing young talent and developing the skills of future golfers. Additionally, the company organizes exceptional corporate and amateur golf events, offering memorable experiences for clients and participants alike. With a commitment to excellence across all its offerings, BSPORT is quickly establishing itself in the Malaysian golf scene.

Programs

BSPORT GOLF, with guidance from the Malaysia Golf Association (MGA), uses a systematic approach to encourage long-term player development.

BSPORT GOLF Development Program is designed to help young players of all skill levels learn, grow, and enjoy the sport of golf. Through four progressive levels Starter, Sport, Player, and Champion . BSPORT offer a structured, goal oriented approach to developing golfers who are not only technically skilled but also love the game.

Each level is carefully designed to build on the previous one, focusing on five core elements that are essential for youth to learn, practice, and master to become better golfers and enjoy their time on the course. These elements are:

All participants will be exposed to the fundamentals of golf including short game, long game, putting, rules and etiquette. To provide a safe and intimate teaching environment, each session will be limited to 20 students with a 5:1 student/teacher ratio. The objective of the program is for each child to learn to play golf and have fun in the process.

  1. Individual & Group Lessons Fexible and specialized lessons to cater individual or group learning requirements.
  2. Junior Player Development Golf Program Structured and progressive learning program designed to cultivate golfing interest of the young and develop the skills of future champions.
  3. Corporate Golf Customized program catered for corporate companies such as golf clinic, golf seminar, team building session through golf and other various golf activities.
  4. National Programs play a pivotal role in identifying and developing young talent, providing them with the tools and support needed to reach their full potential. By offering access to expert coaching, competitive play, and a pathway to international success, these programs help to foster the next generation of golfers who can compete at the highest levels. Whether through grassroots initiatives, high-performance academies, or national team representation, these programs are crucial in elevating the sport of golf across the globe.
